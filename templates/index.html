The stocks game

A simple game where you try to maximize the profit of your stock.

Game rules
(i) You are given a stock with a value of 10 units and 7 days to sell it.
(ii) Each day you are offered a price in the market, and you can choose to sell or not sell your stock.
(iii) On the 7th day you are forced to sell the stock no matter the price.
Remarks
(i) The prices offered at the market follow are random (uniform) between 0 and 20.
(ii) There is another agent playing at the same time, if you both sell the same day you are penalized and only receive half of the price.
(iii) Any day you will know if the other agent already sold his stock. Otherwise, bare in mind to try not to choose the same day to sell.
